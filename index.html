FAQ

How quickly can we see results?

Most clients see improvements within 30-60 days. Lending solutions can be set up in 30 days, while revenue initiatives typically show results within the first quarter.

What industries do you work with?

We specialize in fintech: brokers, funders, lenders, and financial software companies. From startups to established enterprises across all funding verticals.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

All engagements begin with comprehensive NDAs. We maintain strict security protocols and never share client information with competitors.

What's included in full-time consulting?

Dedicated consultant, weekly strategy sessions, 24/7 support, complete business analysis, implementation support, team training, and quarterly reviews.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. All projects include knowledge transfer and documentation. Many clients retain us for ongoing advisory services and additional projects.

Can you provide references?

Absolutely. We can connect you with relevant references based on your needs during our initial consultation.
